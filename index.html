Shakespeare's Much Ado About Nothing

Created by Panagiotis Domvros, English and Drama Teacher

The war was over. Prince Don Pedro was happy. He came to Messina with his good friends, Claudio and Benedick. They were guests of Leonato, the governor. Everyone was celebrating and felt safe...

Leonato had a kind daughter named Hero. She was there to welcome them. A young man named Claudio saw her. He liked her right away. She liked him too.

Beatrice was Hero's cousin. She was very smart and funny. She saw Benedick. Benedick did not want to get married, and Beatrice did not want to get married either. They started a "fun fight" with words. They said funny, clever things to each other.

Later, Claudio told Don Pedro that he loved Hero. "I want to marry her," he said. The Prince liked love stories. He made a plan. That night, at a party with masks, the Prince would wear a costume. He would talk to Hero for Claudio.

But not everyone was happy. Don Pedro's brother, Don John, was not happy. He heard the plan. Don John was a bad man. He wanted to make trouble. He told his friend, Borachio, that he wanted to stop the plan.

At the party, Don John tried to trick Claudio. He told Claudio that the Prince wanted Hero for himself. But it was not true. Soon, Don Pedro brought Hero to Claudio. He said that her father agreed they could get married.

The wedding was in one week. Don Pedro had a new idea. He wanted to trick Benedick and Beatrice. He wanted them to fall in love. Leonato and Claudio wanted to help with this good trick.

First, they made a plan for Benedick. Benedick was hiding in the garden. He was pretending to read. Don Pedro, Claudio, and Leonato talked very loudly. They said that Beatrice loved Benedick very much, but she was too scared to tell him.

Next, it was Beatrice's turn. Hero and her friend, Ursula, walked in the garden. They talked loudly too. They said that poor Benedick was very sad because he loved Beatrice, and she did not love him. Beatrice was hiding nearby. She heard everything.

So, one love story was starting. But another love story was going to have a big problem.

Don John took them to a place under Hero's window. It was dark. They saw a man, Borachio, talking to a woman at the window. The woman was really Hero's friend, Margaret. But Borachio called her "Hero." This was part of the bad plan.

The next day was the wedding. Everything went wrong. The priest asked Claudio if he would marry Hero. Claudio was very angry. "No," he shouted. He told everyone that Hero was bad. He made her feel very ashamed. She was so sad that she fainted.

It was a very bad situation. The wise priest, Friar Francis, had a plan. He said they should tell everyone that Hero died from sadness. Beatrice was very upset. She knew her cousin was a good person. She was alone with Benedick and said something surprising: "Kill Claudio."

But soon, people learned the truth. The local policemen were not very smart, but they were heroes. Their leader was a funny man named Dogberry. They heard Borachio talking. He was drunk. He said that Don John gave him money to trick the Prince. The policemen arrested him.

When everyone knew about the bad plan, Claudio felt very, very bad. Leonato told him what he must do. He must marry Leonato's "niece." This girl looked just like Hero. Claudio wanted to make things right, so he said yes right away.

At the new wedding, the bride wore a veil over her face. When she took it off, it was Hero! She was alive and everyone knew she was good. Claudio cried because he was so happy. Then, Benedick and Beatrice finally said they loved each other. Their friends showed everyone the love poems they wrote. So, there were two weddings that day.

Scenes and Dialogues

Picture 1: The Arrival

Leonato: Welcome to Messina, Prince Don Pedro! We are glad to have you.

Don Pedro: Thank you, Leonato. My friends and I are tired after the war.

Leonato: Please, come inside and rest.

Don Pedro: We are happy to be your guests.

Picture 2: First Glance

Claudio: Benedick, look! Who is that lovely lady?

Benedick: That is Hero, Leonato's daughter.

Cl Claudio: I think she is the most beautiful woman I have ever seen.

Benedick: Oh no, don't fall in love!

Picture 3: A Merry War

Beatrice: Hello, Signior Benedick. Are you still bothering all the ladies?

Benedick: And hello to you, Lady Beatrice. I am surprised you are not fighting with your sharp words.

Beatrice: It is better to have a sharp tongue than a lonely heart.

Picture 4: A Confession

Claudio: My Prince, I have something to tell you.

Don Pedro: What is it, my dear friend?

Claudio: I am in love with Hero. I want to marry her.

Don Pedro: Don't worry. I will help you.

Picture 5: A Villain's Plan

Don John: Did you hear the news? Claudio wants to marry Hero.

Borachio: Yes, my lord. The Prince is helping him.

Don John: We must stop this. I want to make them all unhappy.

Picture 6: The Masked Ball

Don Pedro: My lady, you are very beautiful tonight.

Hero: Thank you, sir. Do I know you?

Don Pedro: I am a friend of a man who loves you very much. His name is Claudio.

Picture 7: Tricking Benedick

Don Pedro: Did you know? Poor Beatrice loves Benedick so much.

Leonato: Yes! But she is too scared to tell him.

Claudio: He would not believe it! He thinks no one can love him.

Picture 8: Tricking Beatrice

Hero: Ursula, did you hear? Poor Benedick is sick with love for Beatrice.

Ursula: It's true! He whispers her name in his sleep.

Hero: She would never believe it. She is too proud.

A Secret Conversation

Picture 9: The Deception

Claudio: Look! It is Hero at her window with a man.

Don Pedro: I cannot believe my eyes.

Claudio: She is not a good person. I will not marry her!

Picture 10: The Wedding Accusation

Claudio: No! I will not marry her.

Leonato: What is wrong? What do you mean?

Claudio: She is not a good person. Ask her what she did last night!

Hero: I don't understand. I did nothing wrong.

Picture 11: The Aftermath

Leonato: My poor child! She is hurt!

Don Pedro: Listen to me. We will tell everyone she is dead.

This will give us time to find the truth.

Leonato: Do you think this plan will work?

Picture 12: A Fierce Demand

Beatrice: My cousin Hero is a good person. Claudio is a liar.

Benedick: I know you are upset.

Beatrice: If you love me, you must challenge Claudio.

Benedick: What? That is a hard thing to ask!

Picture 13: The Arrest

Watchman: We heard him! He said Don John paid him.

Dogberry: You are under arrest! You are a bad man.

Borachio: Let me go! You don't understand anything.

Picture 14: Atonement

Leonato: You killed my daughter with your cruel words.

Claudio: I am so sorry. I made a terrible mistake.

Leonato: To make things right, you must marry my niece. You cannot see her face before you agree.

Picture 15: The Reveal

Claudio: I will marry your niece, to show I am sorry.

Hero: (Takes off veil) I am not a niece. I am Hero!

Claudio: You are alive! Can you ever forgive me?

Benedick: So, Lady Beatrice, will you marry me?

Beatrice: Yes, but only because I feel sorry for you.

Benedick: And I will marry you, but only to save the world.

Beatrice: I think I love you.
